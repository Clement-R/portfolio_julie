Flampage

Flampage is a 2D top-down arena shooter where you play a chef expert in molecular cuisine. But his last dish went wrong and created a giant (and cute) pudding monster! It looks cute, but also tasty!

Protect your monster from hordes of enemies using your custard cannon and heal your monster by shooting it.

Find the right balance between fighting enemies and healing your monster because ammunition is limited! Student game exercise with a team of 6, making an Arena shooter game. The point was to train at making classical game genres

On this project, I worked on the ennemy animations in four directions. I got to understand in depths the puppet tool in After Effects while trying to give life and personnalyty to the characters depending on their types.
