Déjeuner sur l'herbe

You play as two members of a cannibal tribe, living in Bibouh’s forest. Your village is hungry, and demands you to kill and bring back their lunch, a grandma who produces Foie gras, without being noticed.

On this project, I got the opportunity to train myself on 3D modeling and animation with another talented artist and a team of seven people of different backgrounds.
We wanted the game to look silly and cheerful, despite your objective in the game, so you would not feel discomfort while reaching your goal, and feel attached to you community.
Therefore, we chose to diversify the character's silhouettes a lot, giving them comical gestures and felt pen textures.
3 months school project

character design - modelisation - texturing - animation

Cooperative + Stealth
Requires two controlers
