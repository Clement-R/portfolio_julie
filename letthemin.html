Let them in

Let them in is a rythm game settled in a forest, made during Ludum Dare 37. For its theme, "Small room" we decided to flip the view and show that room from the outside.
You use four keys on the keyboard to let feral animals (and a lumberjack) in a small cabin for the greatest party of all times.

For this project I got to work with another graphic designer that made the lights, the scene and the asset integration, while I was modeling and animating all the characters on Maya.
